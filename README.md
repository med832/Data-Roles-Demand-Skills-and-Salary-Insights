## 📋 Project Overview
This project provides a comprehensive analysis of the retail landscape in Nigeria, focusing on the intersection between traditional offline retail and the rapidly growing e-commerce sector. By analyzing 50,000 transactions from 2024, we uncover consumer preferences, spending habits, and the effectiveness of cross-channel marketing strategies.

## 🚀 Key Features
- **Comprehensive Dataset:** Analysis of 50,000 unique transactions across Nigeria.
- **Multi-Channel Scope:** Covers major online platforms (**Jumia, Konga, Jiji, PayPorte, Glovo**) and offline retailers (**Shoprite, Spar, Slot, Computer Village, Game**).
- **Geographic Insights:** Detailed breakdown of sales across 15 major Nigerian cities.
- **Behavioral Analysis:** Comparison of online-only, offline-only, and hybrid "cross-channel" shoppers.
